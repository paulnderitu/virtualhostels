St Marys Girls Hostel

The hostel is ideal for Kenyatta University (KU) Students. Located in Kahawa Sukari, 500km from Kenyatta Sacco, Bungoma Road 2nd North Avenue. The hostel can accommodate 30 students, it is fully furnished with ample security and a caretaker.
• Breakfast, afternoon coffee/tea and dinner served daily • Lunch during weekends and public holidays only • Accommodation is mainly special single rooms • Bed and mattress Accommodation fees - per semester (3months) From May 2016 price:Ksh 3750

Tulivu Hostel for KU Students

Get one room per student, mattress, an elegant closet, own reading table and chair and a well-lit room with an awesome view of the horizon, own cloth line, hot showers, and many more. Tulivu Hostels are located next to the Nyayo hostels so you don't have to walk long distances.
Prices per room: Single (special) – ksh.10,000 per month Two sharing – ksh.8,000 per month Four sharing – ksh.4,500 per month Meals provided: Breakfast Supper


Winsay Hostel in Ruiru

The hostel is ideal for students of Zetech and KU universities. Located 1 kilometer from the campus. The hostel can accommodate 200 students, it has ample security and a caretaker. Offering bed, mattress, water and electricity and food at an extra cost of KShs 7000 per month. Charges:

Facilities: - Internet. - DSTV. - Meals (breakfast & dinner). price:Ksh 3750
